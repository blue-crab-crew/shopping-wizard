Privacy Policy


This Privacy Policy applies to the Shopping Wizard mobile application (the "App"), provided by Blue Crab Crew. We are committed to protecting your privacy.


Information Collection and Use

This App collects a Username from you, which will be stored in our database. You may choose any Username you like.

The app also collects a list of Usernames of Friends (which you specify), and a list of Stores (that you enter) containing Items that can be found while shopping.

Other than your self-selected Username, this app does not collect any personally identifiable information from its users. We do not gather names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other information that could be used to identify you personally.


Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share any collected data with third parties.
