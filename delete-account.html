Deleting Your Account

To delete your account in the Shopping Wizard app, click the settings icon (that looks like a gear), and then choose Delete Account.

Alternatively, you can send an email with the subject "Delete Account" to the following email address: bluecrabcrew@gmail.com. Be sure to let us know either your Username within the app, or the email address associated with your Google Account.

Once your account is deleted, all of the data we have stored for you will be permanently deleted, including:

  • Your Username
  • Your List of Friends
  • All of your Stores

Note that if you previously shared any stores with friends, your friends will no longer be able to access them after your account is deleted.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at bluecrabcrew@gmail.com.
